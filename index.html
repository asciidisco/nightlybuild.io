NightlyBuild 2015

Save the date: 4. September 2015

A hand-crafted after work conference for web enthusiasts loving technology, design and inspiration in Cologne, Germany.

Location

For this year’s event we have chosen a new location.
NightlyBuild 2015 will be held at the Theaterhaus in central Cologne, Germany.

How to find the venue

The venue is located in the center of Cologne Ehrenfeld, one of the neighbourhoods where youth like to meet—with a lot of small cafés, bars, restaurants and clubs. Bahnhof Ehrenfeld is just around the corner and the subway station Körnerstraße (line 3 and 4) is also in 5 minute walking distance.
