Location

The event will be held at the Kölner Künstler Theater in central Cologne, Germany. The nice little theatre with its café will host you during the conference and let you enjoy the evening with all other attendees.

How to find the venue

The venue is located in the center of Cologne Ehrenfeld, one of neighbourhoods where the youth likes to meet with a lot of small cafés, bars, restaurants and clubs.

You can find the Kölner Künstler Theater in Grüner Weg 5, 50825 Köln-Ehrenfeld. Find a map below. If you are coming by public transit you can take the S12 and S13 from Cologne Central Station, track 11, to Köln Ehrenfeld (two stops) and then walk 700 meter to the venue. Please make sure to buy a ticket for about 2,40 Euro. The ride and walk takes about 15 minutes, a train leaves every 10 minutes.
