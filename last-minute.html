We value each and every person who is coming to our event and for the conference we want to get the best experience for everyone. Thus we would kindly ask you to make yourself familiar with our Code of Conduct if you want to attend the conference.

Get there

By plane

If you’re flying to the conference, choose a local train (S-Bahn) to Cologne Central station or Hansaring, then choose the S-Bahn to "Köln Ehrenfeld" and switch off there. From there, the venue is within a walking distance (see below).

By train

Arriving at Cologne Central Station ("Köln Hbf"), choose the S-Bahn (S12, S13) on track 11 (2 stops) to "Köln Ehrenfeld" and switch off there. If you’re taking a local cable car, switch off at "Köln Ehrenfeld / Venloer Str./Gürtel" (Tram lines 3, 4, 13 or Bus 141, 142, 143). From there, the venue is within a walking distance (see below).
Please make sure to buy a train ticket (costs 2.40 Euro). The ride and walk takes about 15 minutes, trains leave every 10 minutes.

Walk

Starting from the train stations mentioned above, walk the Ehrenfeld Gürtel (which is the street where all the small restaurants are) straight on the road to the south. There’s a Burger King on half the way to the venue. It’s a 6 minutes walk from the station and easy to find. Here’s a Link to the venue on Google Maps.
